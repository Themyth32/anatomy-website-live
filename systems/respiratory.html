Respiratory System

The respiratory system is how your body gets oxygen from the air into your blood, and pushes waste carbon dioxide back out. Every time you breathe, this system is working — even while you sleep. It works closely with the circulatory system, which carries the oxygen your lungs collect to the rest of your body.
